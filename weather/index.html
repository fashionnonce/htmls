Introduction

Weather Life (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and secure personal information when you use the Weather Life mobile application.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Location data: Approximate device location is used to provide local weather forecasts and lifestyle suggestions.
  • Device and usage data: Information about your device, app version, and app usage to improve performance and troubleshoot issues.
  • Network data: IP address and connection information required to download weather data and content.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide accurate weather forecasts and lifestyle advice.
  • Personalize app features and local recommendations.
  • Improve app performance and stability.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data in the following cases:

  • With service providers who help us operate the app and deliver weather content.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.
  • To protect our rights, safety, or property, or those of users.

Third-Party Services

Weather Life may use third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and content delivery. These services may collect information independently under their own privacy policies.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no system is completely secure.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable location access in your device settings, but the app may not provide local weather information or personalized advice if location permission is denied.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will revise the effective date above.
